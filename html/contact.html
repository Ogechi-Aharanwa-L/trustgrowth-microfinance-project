Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers Before You Contact Us

Find simple answers to common questions about TrustGrowth loans, savings, applications, and customer support.

How do I apply for a TrustGrowth loan?

You can start by choosing the loan type that fits your need, preparing your basic documents, and speaking with a TrustGrowth advisor for guidance.

Can traders and small business owners apply?

Yes. Traders, vendors, and small business owners can apply with proof of business activity and other required documents.

Can I open a savings plan for a specific goal?

Yes. TrustGrowth supports target savings for school fees, rent, business plans, family goals, and future funds.

How long does it take to get a response?

Response time depends on the enquiry type and working hours, but our team aims to respond as quickly and clearly as possible.

Can I get repayment guidance before applying?

Yes. A TrustGrowth advisor can explain repayment expectations and help you understand what may fit your income flow.

Can I contact TrustGrowth before choosing a service?

Yes. You can contact the team first, explain your need, and get guidance on whether loans, savings, or advisory support is the best starting point.
