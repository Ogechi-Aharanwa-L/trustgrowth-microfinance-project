Yes. Business savings can help traders and SMEs separate business money from personal spending.

Can I save toward a specific target?

Yes. Target savings are useful for school fees, rent, equipment, family plans, and other planned goals.

Can I speak to someone before choosing a plan?

Yes. A TrustGrowth advisor can help you choose the savings plan that fits your income and goal.
